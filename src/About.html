ABOUT

Welcome to ZenPlate! I'm Kaylah, and my love affair with food traces back to my Jamaican roots, where I grew up savoring the aromatic and soulful dishes crafted by my mom. From her perfectly seasoned jerk chicken to the comforting embrace of rice and peas, my mom's kitchen was a treasure trove of flavors that ignited my passion for cooking. When the pandemic hit and life slowed down, I found solace and inspiration in the kitchen. Cooking became my therapy, my creative outlet, and a way to connect with cherished memories of family gatherings and shared meals. But that's not all. At ZenPlate, I understand the challenges of being a college student - balancing studies, budget, and time constraints. That's why I'm dedicated to sharing budget-friendly and healthy recipes that require minimal cooking equipment. Whether you're in a dorm room with just a microwave and a mini-fridge or cooking in a shared kitchen with limited resources, I've got you covered. Let's cook, eat, and savor life's flavorful moments together!
